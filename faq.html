KRIYA 2020

**Download KRIYA 2020 app from playstore for ease of use**


General Instructions

1. All Kriya participants should pay general registration fee.

• PSG Student – Rs 150

• Non PSG Student – Rs 250

2. Workshop Participants will be charged Kriya general registration fee in addition to workshop fee.

3. The participant will pay the Kriya general registration fee only once incase he/she attends more than 1 workshop.

4. Paper Presentation, Poster Presentation & Eureka

• Participants should send their abstracts within the given dead line.

• The shortlisted abstracts will be intimated through mail.

5. Judges decision will be final in all events.


FAQs

1. Who can participate in KRIYA 2020?

• All UG and PG Engineering Students can Participate.

2. Is Workshop onspot registration available?

• Yes, but seats are filled on first come first serve bases so its better to register workshops Online.

3. What is the KRIYA 2020 accommodation policy?

• Since Kriya attracts huge crowd accommodation is filled on first come first serve bases.

4. Who will receive participation certificate?

• All workshop Participants will receive participation certificate

• All Flagship Events Participants will receive Participation Certificate. (except kriya open quiz- only final round participants will receive participation certificate)

• All shortlisted participants of Paper Presentation, Poster Presentation & Eureka will receive participation certificate.

• For all other events Final round participants will receive participation certificate.
